# Scroll Lifecycle Review Scroll

Use this Scroll to review learning proposals and Scroll lifecycle evidence.

## Review checklist

- The proposal is mission-local.
- Evidence paths are explicit.
- The proposal is clearly marked evidence only.
- No official Scroll or doctrine file was modified by the lifecycle tool.
- The review decision is explicit.
- The review decision does not grant execution authority.
- Any promotion plan is plan-only.
- Shikamaru and human approval remain required for doctrine changes.
- Private context was not copied into public examples.
- Mission closure was not implied by a proposal or review.

## Non-authority boundary

Learning proposals, review records, indexes, and promotion plans do not authorize execution, model calls, adapter calls, repository apply, Git operations, private context access, doctrine rewrite, or mission closure.
