# Teachback Protocol Gate

A Konoha mission is not complete until the human can explain:

1. what was done;
2. why it was done;
3. how to defend it;
4. what evidence supports it;
5. what remains blocked or out of scope.

## Exact confirmation

The closure gate requires:

```text
I_CAN_EXPLAIN_AND_DEFEND_THIS_MISSION
```

This exact phrase is a deliberate friction point.

## Boundary

Teachback does not authorize future execution.

Teachback is a closure condition, not an execution permission.
