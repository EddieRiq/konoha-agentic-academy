# ADR-0004: Supervised doctrine evolution

## Status

Accepted for v3.6.0 constitutional baseline.

## Context

Konoha should improve from mission evidence without allowing self-modifying
agents or silent policy drift.

## Decision

Shikamaru may draft instruction deltas from evidence. An independent reviewer
must evaluate them. Hokage may recommend acceptance within its authority, and
the human user must approve permanent doctrine changes when policy requires it.

## Consequences

- No autonomous doctrine mutation.
- Every permanent change is traceable to evidence and approvals.
- A model cannot modify its own governing instructions.
