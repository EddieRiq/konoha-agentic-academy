# ADR-0002: Bounded role model

## Status

Accepted for v3.6.0 constitutional baseline.

## Context

Konoha depends on specialized roles with independent oversight.

## Decision

Every role has explicit responsibility, authority, tools, context, budget and
escalation path. No role may silently substitute for another, approve its own
work, expand its authority or modify its own governing constraints.

## Consequences

- Hokage decides strategy but does not execute.
- Workers execute bounded assignments.
- Jounin reviews independently.
- Shikamaru proposes doctrine deltas but cannot apply or approve them.
