## Safety model

Konoha does not self-approve, mutate doctrine autonomously or treat model output
as permission. Mission Charter, action, review, Teachback, Git and release gates
remain separate.

## Known limitations

- Subscription limits and renewal dates may require manual entry.
- Monetary cost remains unknown unless explicit provider pricing is supplied.
- Provider readiness depends on locally installed and authenticated CLIs.
- GPU discovery is limited when `nvidia-smi` is unavailable.
